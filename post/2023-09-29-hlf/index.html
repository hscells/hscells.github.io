    Attending the 2023 Heidelberg Laureate Forum

    Sep 29, 2023 1 min read

    I was selected to attend the 2023 Heidelberg Laureate Forum as a young researcher in the field of computer science. The event was held from September 24 - 29, 2023 in Heidelberg, Germany. Attendees included young researchers and laureates of the Abel Prize, ACM A.M. Turing Award, ACM Prize in Computing, Fields Medal, IMU Abacus Medal and Nevanlinna Prize.

    The event was a unique mix of academic and cultural exploration, fostering interdisciplinary discussions and encouraging collaboration. Connecting with laureates and fellow young researchers, the forum left a lasting impact on me, providing inspiration and valuable connections.

    I can absolutely recommend anyone interested to apply!

    Harry Scells
    Postdoctoral Research Fellow
