Demos

Input image


This is a photograph I took in 2014. It is at Palm Beach, which is about an hour north of Sydney.

Results


Starry Starry Night - Van Gogh
The huge range of small strokes make this style of painting very easy for the AI to replicate. Many of this artists paintings produce a very nice output.

Circus Sideshow - Georges Seurat
It was hoped that the pointilism style would be replicated in the output, however, it seems this style of painting is just too much for the AI.

Ode to Joy - Craig Graci
Because the blocks of colour here are relativly small and close together, the AI was able to paint a reasonable version of it. A bad example of this is demonstrated further below.

Pacman - Atari
As expected, the outline of the game board has such a significant detail that the main focus of the resulting image is this.

The Scream - Edvard Munch
A main component of the scream is the bridge, and the AI has picked up on this feature, and it is very visible in the result.

Mona Lisa - Da Vinci
Due to how the AI interpreted the backgrounds and how the colours of Mona's dress are very dark and similar, there are not many features being detected here. As such, the painting lacks detail and the stokes are large and wild.

The Gate - Hans Hoffman
The AI is unable to detect filled in poritions of the painintg, however is does outline the regions where boxes are.
